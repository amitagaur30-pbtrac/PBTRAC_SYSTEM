Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Who can file a complaint before PTAC?

Any citizen who has applied for a notified public service and has faced delay, non-delivery, rejection or deficiency in service can file a complaint before the Commission.

2. What information is required while filing a complaint?

The following details are mandatory:

  • Name and Address of Applicant
  • Mobile Number and Email ID
  • Name of Department and Office Concerned
  • Name of Service Applied For
  • Application/Acknowledgment Number
  • Date of Submission of Application
  • Brief Description of Grievance

3. What documents should be attached?

Citizens should upload copies of:

  • Original application submitted to the department
  • Acknowledgment receipt
  • Any correspondence with the department
  • Proof of delay, rejection or deficiency, if available

4. What relief can be sought from the Commission?

The applicant may seek:

  • Direction for delivery of the service
  • Action against the concerned official
  • Imposition of penalty for delay
  • Compensation or any other appropriate relief

5. Is there any fee for filing a complaint?

No. Filing a complaint before PTAC through this portal is free of cost.

6. How can I track my complaint?

After successful registration, a unique Complaint Number will be generated. Use this number under the "Track Complaint" section.

7. Can I upload multiple documents?

Yes. Multiple supporting documents can be uploaded in PDF, JPG, JPEG and PNG formats.

8. What happens after filing the complaint?

After submission, the complaint is scrutinized by PTAC officials. The complaint may then be assigned for examination and further action.

9. Will I be able to see remarks or status updates?

Yes. Citizens can view the latest status and remarks entered by Commission officials through the Track Complaint section.

10. What types of grievances should NOT be filed?

Complaints unrelated to notified public services, personal disputes, court matters, or anonymous complaints should not be filed through this portal.
